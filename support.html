Support

At AutoPlex, we're committed to providing you with top-tier customer support. Whether you need help navigating our website, have questions about a specific vehicle, or want to learn more about the buying and selling process, we're here to assist you.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I list my car for sale?
    Follow the 'Sell Your Car' link in our main menu. If you need more detailed instructions, check out our step-by-step guide.
  • How do I schedule a test drive?
    Each vehicle listing has a 'Schedule Test Drive' button. Simply click this button and fill out the form, and our team will get back to you promptly.
  • How do I know the listed cars are in good condition?
    We require all sellers to provide detailed information and images of their vehicles. We also encourage buyers to arrange a pre-purchase inspection for additional peace of mind.
